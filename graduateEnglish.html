University Students’ Different Online Shopping Interests
As we can see in the chart, there are some differences between male and female university students in terms of online shopping. Compared with the male students’ almost even interest in clothing, books, electronic products and game currency, female students prefer clothing and books to e-products and computer games.

The data in the chart can be interpreted as follows: female students love reading and clothing far more than male students. As a matter of fact, there are great differences between men and women’s shopping preferences. First of all, regarding personal character, men like adventures, by which they enjoy excitement; while women tend to be quiet and enjoy peaceful time. This can partly explain the shopping interest differences between male and female students. Moreover, in the light of biological factor, the thinking patterns of men are logical, so electronic products and computer games can meet their needs. Women, however, are more sensitive and can easily be attracted by fashion. On the whole, the preferences in shopping express the basic differences between men and women in character.

What Kind of Mobile Phones Do Students Want?
The chart provides some data regarding elements in decision making when students buy mobile phones. Stable signal and multi-function are the top two factors which affect the students’ choices.
重写
译
As we all know, students seldom care too much about power saving, practicability and durability of their mobile phones. There are mainly two reasons why they pay less attention to these factors. First, making calls is not the only function of a cellphone. Students want to surf the Internet, play games, chat with friends and watch videos with their phones. Therefore, it is really advantageous for a mobile phone to bear more functions. Second, a multi-functional mobile phone will be totally useless if it is not connected to the network. We often see people holding their phones high in the air to search for signals, some of them are even driven mad by bad signal. Therefore, students would like to consider function and signal first when they buy mobile phones.

译
From what has been discussed above, students do establish a positive view of consumption. When they buy phones, they consider more about the functions of a cellphone as a communication tool.

How Do College Students Spend Their Spare Time?
The graph presents the general trend of how college students spend their spare time. More than half of college students spend their spare time listening to music. Watching TV series, going shopping and taking part in after-class activities are also the common choices. A small number of students choose to play computer games or go on a trip.
重写
译
As can be seen from the graph, students today have more ways to relax themselves, with music as the irreplaceable number one. To begin with, all people like music, college students are not exceptions. With the development of IT, music and video players are made smaller and smaller and it is easier for students to download the music and video they like from the Internet. Therefore, it is not strange to see young people wearing fancy headphones. Next, because of the improvement of living standard, students also try to find more chances to know the world and the society. Thus, they take part in more activities and travel more than ever before. In conclusion, they are pursuing more colorful life than before.

译
It is by no means a good thing to listen to music or play mobile phones too much in one’s spare time. It needs the joint efforts of the school and the society to provide students with more opportunities to get involved in this world.
